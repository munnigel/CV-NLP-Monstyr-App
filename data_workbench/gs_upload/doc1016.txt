A fantastical candy wonderland awaits at Singapore Changi AIrport with the Chupa Chups pop-up. From Chupa Chups lolly sculptures to a colourful candy playground, this showcase is one for kids of all ages. Snap a picture in front of the giant lollipop, which measures 11.1m long and 4m tall, holding Singapore's Book of Records for the largest Chupa Chups lollipop display in the city. Located less than 30 minutes from the hotel by car, check out this limited-time series of candylicious Chupa Chups displays from now till 3 May 2022.
