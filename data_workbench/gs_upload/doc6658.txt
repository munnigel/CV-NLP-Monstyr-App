Opera Gallery Singapore is delighted to present "Fernando Botero", an exclusive solo exhibition of more than twenty sculptures and paintings, on view from 22 April to 15 May 2022. Born in Medellin in 1932, Fernando Botero is one of the most famous contemporary Latin American artists alive. Best known for his paintings and sculptures of smooth inflated shapes, Botero constantly searches to give volume presence and reality by using exaggerated and voluptuous forms. Experience Botero's universe of enigmatic shapes and forms at Opera Gallery, ION Orchard (02-16).
