Announcement: We are pleased to announce NX Field Firmware Ver. 1.1.0 that adds support for Nikon Z 7II, Z 6II, Z 7 and Z 6 mirrorless cameras is here. The latest update enables remote operation of silent More shooting, a unique function for mirrorless cameras. Additionally, it provides added support for Face- and Eye-detection AF, ensuring a more reliable focus on your subject while shooting remotely. Learn more about the latest update here: bit.ly/NXField_v1_10 *Support for Z 9 is planned for future update.
