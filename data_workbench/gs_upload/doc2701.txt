MR-G Hana-Basara The ultimate in metal form, created with leading-edge technology and time-honoured artisanship. This trailblazing watch was specially commissioned to the making of an actual, one-of-a-kind More MR-G Hana-Basara suit of armour based on the Basara commander spirit. Employs an ultra-hard COBARION and DAT55G alloys developed in Japan and a cutting/polishing artisan to apply techniques rarely used in metalwork, achieving mirror-surface facet cuts in metal. Now available at ION Orchard and Marina Bay Sands.
