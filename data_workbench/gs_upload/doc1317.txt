It is a long-standing Chinese tradition for families to gather for a feast during Chinese New Year. It symbolizes the togetherness of a family as the old saying goes, "the family that eats together, stays together". This Chinese New Year, we will be releasing a special stamp series, featuring the popular dish "Pen Cai", which is known for comprising a sumptuous mix of ingredients within one dish. This limited stamp series will be available at all post offices, philatelic stores, and our online store, https://shop.singpost.com from 11 Feb onwards.
