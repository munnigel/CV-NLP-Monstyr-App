From now till 25 June, experience the season of Spring through the paintings of renowned local artists, Mdm Kwei Chin Pen and Mdm Lim Fei, at the "Nanyang Finger Ink Art" exhibition by Gallery Na Wei. Known for their finger art paintings, both Mdm Kwei and Mdm Lim perfectly depict the beauty and essence of blooming and beginning through their fine works. Part of the proceeds from the artwork sales will go towards supporting Singapore Thong Chai Medical Institution.
