A bigger, brighter Constellation. OMEGA Watches will be rolling out new additions to their Constellation collection in 36mm with 1.11ct of diamonds adorning the bezel and indices. Variants include stainless steel with burgundy dial, 18K yellow gold with blue dial and 18K Sedna gold with brown dial. Each comes with a matching crocodile leather strap and is powered by OMEGA's Co-Axial Master Chronometer Caliber 8800. #OMEGA #Constellation
