As the Mid-Autumn Festival comes around the corner, we revisit the significance of this ancient Chinese tradition. The most popular myth revolves around the hero archer, Hou Yi, and his beautiful wife, Chang E. More As the legend goes, after saving the earth from drought and disaster by shooting down nine out of the ten suns, Hou Yi was rewarded with an elixir which will ascend him to heaven. However, when his apprentice attempted to steal the elixir, Chang E swallowed it out of desperation. Reluctant to leave her husband, she flew to the moon, the nearest place to the earth in heaven.
