Explore the beauty of art through the creative works of talented artists from Rainbow Centre's Artability Programme, brought to life with cosmetics and paint. Held at the East Garden Gallery at The Fullerton More Hotel Singapore from now till 28 February 2022, the "Beauty of Art" exhibition features 38 artworks created with upcycled Shiseido cosmetics, exploring themes of nature, botany, wildlife, fashion, beauty, and Singapore landmarks. This exhibition is supported by Shiseido Asia Pacific.
