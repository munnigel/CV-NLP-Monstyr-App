Coming up: a set of commemorative stamps to celebrate the Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympics. Officially known as the Games of the XXXII Olympiad, the 2020 Summer Olympics will be held in Tokyo, Japan from 23 July 2021 More to 8 August 2021. The set of six stamps features some of the sports that Singapore athletes will be participating in for the upcoming Games. They include: swimming, shooting, sailing, diving, badminton and table tennis. Available from 23rd July, interested collectors can purchase from any post offices, Philatelic Stores, or on our online shop at https://shop.singpost.com/
