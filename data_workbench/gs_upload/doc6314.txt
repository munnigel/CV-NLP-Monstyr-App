In conjunction with Go Skateboarding Day 2022, Vans is bringing Sidewalk Sessions to Singapore for everyone to enjoy; skaters and non-skaters alike! The first ever Vans Sidewalk Sessions in Singapore will be held to celebrate our thriving and colorful local skate scene, providing a platform where skateboarders of all levels and genres can get together on this fun-filled day doing what they love. Let's make it a skate date on 25th June 2022, Silver Garden- at Gardens by the bay. From skate clinics to skate challenges - all in one place and it's open for public!
