Aesop introduces Rinse and Return, a cosmetic packaging recycling initiative. Customers are invited to return cleaned aluminium tubing, glass or plastic containers to Aesop Suntec City, where it will be More transferred for recycling. From now till 31 December, Aesop will donate $1 for every Aesop container collected to Zero Waste SG. Check in-store for details. You may use our Interactive Directory on your mobile phone to find your way to your favourite shop, brand or product. Visit http://quickguide.sunteccity.com.sg.
