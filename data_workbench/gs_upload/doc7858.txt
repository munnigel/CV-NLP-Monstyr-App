New to Club21: ALAIA Since the passing of the uncompromising icon that is Azzedine Alaia, creative director Pieter Mulier has been entrusted the keys to the fashion house. Alaia's distinctive plural visions of More femininity is continued with new techniques in sinuous dresses, draped hoods and flippy black skirts to bring ultra-glamour to body-conscious dressing. Now at Four Seasons.
