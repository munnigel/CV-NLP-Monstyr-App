Love small animals, especially cute kittens? Well, how fur-tunate, because a bunch of these adorable cats have arrived at Our Tampines Hub! A collaboration by the Malay Heritage Centre, Geylang Serai Heritage More Gallery and Japanese artist Juno (@mofu_sand), the travelling exhibition Pawverbs on the Move showcases the delightful world of cats through their cat-terrific place in Malay proverbs and literary culture. Come on down and check out the exhibition showcase at OTH Festive Walk from now to 8 May!
