Widely acclaimed by critics, returns as a performed reading for three nights only after captivating audiences with its poignant stories of love and unlikely beginnings. Director Claire Wong revisits this tender piece with the exceptional cast of Rebekah Sangeetha Dorai and Isabella Chiam, who are accompanied by an exquisite soundscape that moves the heart and lifts the spirit. Come and experience firsthand this classic and beautiful play by Huzir Sulaiman. 5 - 7 April 2022 Esplanade Recital Studio https://bit.ly/3JcAQrK
