Meitenkai Fair "Meiten" () is a Japanese term that translates to "Famous Shop" and is given to reputable brands that have stood the test of time. From 13 to 26 May, head down to the Isetan Scotts More Supermarket to experience this uniquely Japanese experience and enjoy over 65 different products from 9 Meiten food brands like Aji no Hamato, Chaya Macrobiotics, Hayashi kyuemon and more! #isetansg #isetanscotts #isetansupermarket
