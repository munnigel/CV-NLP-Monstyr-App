The Hacker Project, first presented within Gucci Aria, merges the iconic silhouettes and codes of Balenciaga and Gucci in unique creations as an exploration on ideas of authenticity and appropriation within the More fashion industry. Part of a four-chapters campaign for the holidays, The Hacker Project stand out in a film and images by Harley Weir, appropriately appropriated from a past Balenciaga advertising campaign.
